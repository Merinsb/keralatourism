Districts in Kerala

Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram is the capital city of Kerala.

Kochi

Kochi is a major port city on the southwest coast of India.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode is known for its historic sites and pristine beaches.

Thrissur

Thrissur is known as the cultural capital of Kerala.

Kollam

Kollam is known for its cashew processing and coir production.

Alappuzha

Alappuzha is famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises.

Palakkad

Palakkad is known for its scenic beauty and historical significance.
