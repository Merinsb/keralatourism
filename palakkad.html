Palakkad

Palakkad, also known as Palghat, is a city located in the state of Kerala, India. It serves as the administrative headquarters of the Palakkad district. Situated near the border with Tamil Nadu, Palakkad is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and natural beauty. Palakkad offers a range of tourist attractions including the Silent Valley National Park, Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary, Malampuzha Dam and Gardens, and Nelliampathy Hill Station. These places attract nature lovers, wildlife enthusiasts, and adventure seekers alike.
